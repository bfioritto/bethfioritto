Lux Cantorum

Lux Cantorum is the premiere sacred choral music ensemble in Chicago. Formerly the Lutheran Choir of Chicago, Lux Cantorum underwent a name change to better align with their current values, vision, and membership. Although the choir repertoire and membership is longer Lutheran, they have a 70 year history of sacred choral music, and it was important for them to preserve some continuity with this tradition.
