Logo Design

Swift Kick

Consultant and Trainer Kevin Griffin wanted to expand his business, and his previous moniker—Kevin Griffin Consulting—no longer made sense for the goals and direction of his business. The visuals I developed for Swift Kick reflect the owner’s demeanor: playful and approachable. And that's what sets this business apart: when you think software training, you probably think stodgy and boring (Raise your hand if you want to go to a software training!) The tongue-in-cheek, illustrative logo sets Swift Kick apart from business as usual.

I also created a brand “starter kit” for Swift Kick by specifying colors and type choices to help the client maintain consistency across products, marketing materials, and website.
