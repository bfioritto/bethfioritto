Logo Design

Swift Kick

Consultant and Trainer Kevin Griffin wanted to expand his business, and his previous moniker—Kevin Griffin Consulting—no longer made sense for the goals and direction of his business. The visuals I developed for Swift Kick reflect the owner’s demeanor: playful and approachable. Most new work comes from one-on-one interactions with potential clients, so it was essential that the brand fit well with who he is in person.

The logo needed to work in a variety of situations, so we developed variations optimized for different applications and outputs. I also created a brand “starter kit” by specifying colors and type choices to help the client maintain consistency across products, marketing materials, and website.
